Kevin W. Zhang


is currently

  • a 3rd year CS PhD student @ University of Maryland, College Park,

  • coadvised by Christopher Metzler and Jia-Bin Huang,

  • who applies computer vision and computer graphics techniques to computational imaging/sensing research,

and previously

  • graduated with a BA in Computer Science and Pure Mathematics (double major) from the University of California, Berkeley,

  • where he researched computational imaging mentored by Michael Kellman and Jon Tamir under Laura Waller and Michael Lustig at Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research.
